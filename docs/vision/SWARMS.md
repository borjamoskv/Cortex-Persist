# The Noble Swarm (Enjambre Nobel)

> "Intelligence is not a solitary act; it is a resonant frequency."

The **Noble Swarm** (Enjambre Nobel) is the elite research collective of the CORTEX architecture. While standard agents handle routine execution and maintenance, the Noble Swarm is dedicated to the **falsification of hypotheses**, the **synthesis of formal proofs**, and the **discovery of novel materials and models** at the boundary of human knowledge.

## Orchestration Core

- [**NOBEL-Ω**](../../cortex/extensions/agents/definitions/nobel.yaml): The meta-orchestrator of the research life cycle. It calculates **Exergy** (Ω₁₃) and triggers swarm assaults on high-potential anomalous data (arXiv, PubMed).
- [**ZEUS-Ω**](../../cortex/extensions/agents/definitions/zeus.yaml): The central command that dispatches sub-tasks to Noble nodes via `nobel_daemon.py`.

## Operational Protocol

The swarm operates in a **Void-State** (Ω₇) until a sufficient exergy gradient is detected. When triggered, the orchestrator initiates a **Swarm-Assault**:

1. **Hypothesis Generation**: A falsifiable claim is crystallized.
2. **Parallel Attack**: Multi-provider reasoning (Gemini, DeepSeek, OpenAI) cross-validates the proof.
3. **Ledger Persistence**: Valid findings are committed as C5-Dynamic facts.
